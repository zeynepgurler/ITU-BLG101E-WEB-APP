Istanbul Ataturk High School of Science

Istanbul Ataturk High School of Science

Istanbul Ataturk High School of Science was established in 1982 as part of the High School of Science Project as a request of Kenan Evren, president of time. It is located in a 40-acre garden adjacent to the Marmara University campus, where a hunting lodge belonging to Murat V. is located in the 17th century. The school building was previously used as the Istanbul Ataturk Education Institute.

In the garden, besides the school building, there is a separate gym and a theater hall. Also, our school has two dormitories for girls and boys separately. Boys dormitory has been rebuilt by the renewal of the school building.

Seyfi Teoman Short Movie Contest

Since 2017, the Seyfi Teoman Short Movie Competition is organized between high school students by Ataturk High School of Science students in memory of Seyfi Teoman, the award-winning movie director who graduated from Ataturk High School of Science in 1995. Summer Book and Our Grand Despair were his well-known movies. You can check the competitions of the previous years from the official website.

Achievements

Success of getting into a university

Istanbul Ataturk High School of Science, which gave its first graduates in 1985 with great success, especially in the university entrance exam, since then and has always been one of Turkey's leading high schools.

Each year, our graduates achieve the highest scores in the university entrance examination and enroll in the programs such as engineering, medicine, and basic sciences of the best universities in Turkey. Besides these fields, some graduates choose law, management, business, or such in recent years. Their successful career becomes an inspiration to young souls.

In recent years, 60% of our graduates enroll in medicine while 30% of them choosing engineering programs. Also, 70% of our graduates enroll in Turkey's best five universities in their field. Although students generally go with studying in Turkey, some go abroad for licentiate degree choosing University of California, University of Colorado, Nottingham University and such. Science Olimpics

Each year, successful students of our school return with medals in branches like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer from the National Science Olympics organized by TUBITAK. In the last five years alone, Ataturk High School of Science students won 20 gold, 7 silver, and 18 bronze medals at the Olympics. Students of Ataturk High School of Science start their Olympic adventures in the first year. They are being present in competitions even in the first year and win awards.
